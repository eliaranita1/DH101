LA Neighborhoods

For my map I have plotted all of the Airbnb listings within Los Angeles County. I have also used Geojson data from our dataset to delineate the different cities/neighborhoods within LA. If you hover the mouse over an area it will tell you the name of the neighborhood in the info panel at the top right of the map. If you click within that area it will zoom into that neighborhood. When you click the markers for the individual listings it will show the listing info along with a photograph which links to the actual listing on the Airbnb site.

The data shown includes price, rating, and also location which can provide a better visual understanding of where the majority of listings are and their relative prices. It is clear by looking at the map that there are much more listings in west LA than there are on the eastside, with downtown being a boundary between the densely packed listings and the more spread out ones. This makes sense considering that, in general, west LA tends to be higher income areas where people might have more resources to own income properties. From the map we can also compare listings to their proximity to tourist attractions, schools, airports, etc.. to try to find patterns.
