Mean price of properties by neighborhood 




                        Although initially intended to be a service for renting out an extra room from a 
                resident’s home, many renters in the Airbnb have economy have instead turned to renting 
                out their entire home, forgoing the property as their primary residence, and in some cases 
                creating a “business” or turning properties into permanent listings in ways that mimic 
                hotelling services. While every local governance varies, generally, legislation does not 
                have the proper infrastructure to regulate such Airbnb’s in a way that will not harm the 
                local market. The mean of the price of listings by neighborhoods give a sense of not only 
                the amount of revenue being generated in each neighborhood but also the general 
                socio-economic status of the neighborhood.
