Our team investigates the effects of the online lodging marketplace, Airbnb, and its presence in large metropolitan areas, specifically focusing on Los Angeles for our case study. This project seeks to inform Airbnb users of how the service can affect the area they are visiting, as well as uncover the qualities of neighborhoods most likely to be affected by heavy Airbnb traffic.
