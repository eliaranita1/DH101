Sum of reviews by neighborhood 




                Besides any impacts on the local housing markets, frequent use of Airbnb’s in an area can 
        still affect the community, for example, disturbances from excess noise, or general issues of 
        guests not being informed of local rules or practices. The aggregation of reviews by neighborhood 
        shows the neighborhoods which receive the most amount of traffic from Airbnb, a visualization that 
        differs from just the raw presence of Airbnb’s in an area, though this may have an effect on the 
        number of reviews. This represents areas that are desirable for travel by tourists, or at the 
        least where most tourists would like to stay when visiting the Los Angeles area.
