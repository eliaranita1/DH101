Listing Types 





                Under the assumption that a property with greater than 95 days of availability per year 
        is a property that is not the primary residence of the renters, this visualization shows 
        the neighborhoods that are most affected by Airbnb’s being used solely to generate revenue. 
        In particular, these kinds of properties are harmful to the housing market because they 
        are properties that could instead be allocated to people who are looking for long-term 
        housing in the area. Additionally, the reduction in supply of houses in the market is one 
        contributor of housing prices in neighborhoods, a problem which particularly affects those 
        who are in need of low-income housing in high demand areas.
